Recognition on the Forbes 100 Most Creative in Business List 2023

I am honored to be recognized by Forbes as one of the 100 most creative people in business in 2023. As the founder of KIOAI and creator of WatsomApp, my focus is on using robotics and artificial intelligence to address one of the most pressing problems in our society: bullying.

Through KIOAI, we develop humanoid robots that employ artificial intelligence to prevent and detect bullying. This innovation has given rise to WatsomApp, an interactive platform that combines an online game with challenging activities and questions that promote fundamental values such as tolerance and respect.
