At WatsomApp, we are dedicated to transforming school life through the use of advanced artificial intelligence. Our platform employs cognitive technologies to proactively identify and prevent bullying in educational settings.

Technology for an environmentsafer school

As pioneers in the application of AI in education in Spain, our goal is to empower teachers and counselors with tools that enable them to detect and address bullying effectively. Through innovative solutions, we facilitate the understanding of student dynamics, promoting safer and healthier school environments.

We strive to improve school climate by integrating cutting-edge technologies that provide educators with accurate and efficient resources to identify and prevent bullying. With a firm commitment to continuous improvement, WatsomApp is leading the change in school climate management.
